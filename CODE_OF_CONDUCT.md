# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

pwa.mobi is an open community for developers building Progressive Web Apps.
We are committed to making participation in this community a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Submit apps that are real, functional, and honestly described
- Engage constructively with feedback from the audit process
- Help other developers in discussions
- Report issues honestly and in good faith

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Submitting malware, phishing apps, or deceptive software
- Impersonating other apps, developers, or brands
- Harassment of any community member
- Attempting to game or circumvent the audit process
- Submitting the same app multiple times under different names

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. Submission rejection
2. Removal of existing listings
3. Permanent ban from the registry

Reports can be submitted by opening an issue with the `conduct-report` label,
or by emailing conduct@pwa.mobi.

---

*This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces associated with pwa.mobi,
including this repository, Discord, and any other official channels.*
